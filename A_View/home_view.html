About Wave to Earth

Wave to Earth is an indie rock band from South Korea, consisting of three members : Daniel Kim as guitarist and vocalist, Shin Donggyu as drummer, and John Cha as bassist. Formed in 2019, they have released some singles, EPs, and albums as you can see in the discography page.

At first, when Daniel Kim and Shin Donggyu were in middle school, they started this band with the intention of creating songs that would bring "a new wave". And then, they officially debuted on August 23, 2019 including the new member, John Cha.

Although based in South Korea, most of Wave to Earth's songs are written in English. They aim to expand their reach and connect not only with Koreans, but also with global listeners.
